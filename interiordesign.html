Modern Interior Design

Modern interior design is all about simplicity, functionality, and clean lines.
- Neutral Color Palette: Whites, blacks, grays, and beiges create a calm and sleek atmosphere.
- Minimalist Furniture: Think sleek sofas, minimalist coffee tables, and functional decor.
- Natural Light: Maximize sunlight with large windows and mirrors.
- Smart Tech: Integrate tech like smart lighting, thermostats, and security systems.
- Art and Sculptures: Add personality with statement art pieces or sculptures.
- Plains and Patterns: Mix textures with plain surfaces and subtle patterns.
- Scandinavian Modern: Emphasizes simplicity, functionality, and natural materials.
- Industrial Modern: Exposed brick, metal accents, and edgy vibes.
- Mid-Century Modern: Retro-inspired furniture with clean lines and organic shapes.
